General Information

Chuchucrochet specializes in handmade crochet items. Each piece is crafted individually, so slight variations in size, colors and texture make every item unique.

Orders

All orders are subject to availability. Custom orders may require additional time and production. The estimated timeline will be communicated during ordering.

Payment Method

Payment is required in full for on-hand items when placing an order. Accepted methods include GCash, Metrobank, and BDO.

Shipping And Delivery

Orders ship after payment confirmation. Shipping fees depend on destination and weight. We’re not responsible for carrier delays.

Returns And Refunds

No returns or exchanges (items are handmade). If damaged, contact us within 1 day with photos for review.
