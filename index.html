General Information

At ChuchuCrochet, every piece is lovingly handmade. No two items are exactly alike — each creation is unique, soft, and full of personality.

Orders

All products depend on availability. Some items are made-to-order and may take extra time to prepare. You’ll be notified of timelines during checkout.

Payment

We accept GCash, Metrobank, and BDO. For custom orders, a small non-refundable deposit may be required before we begin crafting your item.

Shipping & Delivery

Orders ship after payment confirmation. Fees depend on destination and weight. We’ll provide tracking once shipped.

Returns & Refunds

Since items are handmade, returns aren’t accepted. However, if your order arrives damaged, contact us within 24 hours with photos for review.

Custom Orders

We love bringing your ideas to life! Once production starts, no changes can be made, so double-check your request before confirming.

Care Tips

Gently handwash with mild detergent and air dry. Store in a cool, dry place to keep your crochet creations soft and vibrant.
